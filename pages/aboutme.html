Who Am I?

My name is Emerson Albert! Don't forget it, I might be important to you one day. I was born and raised in Miami, Florida. Where you can experience all four seasons in one day and the nightlife never stops. I'm on my way to becoming a Full Stack Web Developer but the main goal is to be a Software Engineer.

My story starts at the University of Central Florida, where I began diving into the world of coding. The more lines I typed, the stronger the passion grew. The ability to create something from just your imagination is very powerful and we as coders are able to do that with just a couple of keystrokes. I am honored to be apart of any team or client that I will be representing. Lets get to work!

Outside of sitting in front of my laptop, I like to play basketball and play video games. I don't mind schooling someone on the court or online. Both of these hobbies taught be how to perfect my craft and to utilize teamwork and thats that I strive to show in the content that I produce.
